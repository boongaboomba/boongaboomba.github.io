Make a Reservation

At Ezo Sushi Bar & Kitchen, booking a table is simple and convenient. Guests can easily make a reservation online by selecting their preferred date, time, and number of guests using our built-in calendar system. After entering basic contact information, click Confirm Reservation to finalize your booking. A confirmation message will be sent instantly by email or text. For same-day reservations or parties larger than six, please call us directly at (631) 555-9032.
