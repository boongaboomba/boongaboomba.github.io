About the owners of Ezo’s Sushi Bar & Kitchen The partners have developed a vision together: a restaurant that showcases excellent Japanese sushi and introduces Vietnamese and Thai dishes to their suburban Long Island community, which currently has limited Asian dining options beyond Chinese takeout and hibachi chains. Michael brings deep knowledge of Japanese cuisine and cultural context, while Daniel contributes his training in Vietnamese and Thai cooking. Both are invested in doing this respectfully and authentically.

Meet the owners



Michael Chen is Japanese-American (third generation, sansei). Trained under several master sushi chefs in Los Angeles and Tokyo before working in restaurant management.



Daniel spent five years studying culinary traditions throughout Asia. Including formal training in Japanese kaiseki cuisine in Kyoto and working in professional kitchens in Vietnam and Thailand.
