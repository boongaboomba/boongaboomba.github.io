EZO Sushi Bar & Kitchen

Welcome to Ezo Sushi Bar Kitchen. Our mission is the authentic creation of region-specific Asian cuisine.

Our inspiration is to spread the flavors of traditional Asian meals and bring them here with a modern flare — blending the cultural aspects of Japanese, Vietnamese, and Thai cuisine.
